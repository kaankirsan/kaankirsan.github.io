Project Overview

Challenge

Industrial resistance control and power distribution systems require precise monitoring, real-time adjustments, and robust safety mechanisms. Traditional manual control methods are inefficient, prone to errors, and lack the precision needed for modern manufacturing processes. Industries need automated solutions that ensure consistent power delivery, energy efficiency, and comprehensive system monitoring.

Solution

Developed comprehensive PLC-based resistance automation and power control systems integrating Siemens PLCs, custom HMI interfaces, and advanced power electronics. Implemented real-time monitoring with data logging, automated control algorithms for optimal performance, and safety interlocks ensuring operator and equipment protection. Modbus communication enables seamless integration with existing factory systems.

Technical Approach

System architecture centers on Siemens S7-1200/1500 PLCs providing robust control logic with millisecond-level response times. Custom HMI screens display real-time power consumption, resistance values, temperature monitoring, and system status. Advanced PID control algorithms maintain precise resistance values even under varying load conditions.

Power electronics integration includes thyristor controllers, contactors, and transformers managed by PLC logic. Multi-channel data acquisition systems monitor voltage, current, power factor, and thermal parameters. Comprehensive alarm management with predictive maintenance alerts minimizes downtime and extends equipment lifespan.
