Autonomous Solar Panel Cleaning Robot

Architected full-stack robotics solution featuring autonomous navigation with real-time obstacle detection and avoidance using OAK-D depth cameras and SLAM algorithms. Implemented computer vision pipeline for solar panel detection achieving 95%+ accuracy using YOLOv8 with custom training dataset and transfer learning. Designed mobile application for real-time telemetry monitoring, mission planning, and remote robot control with cloud integration.

Project Overview

Challenge

Solar panel efficiency degrades significantly due to dust, dirt, and debris accumulation, reducing energy output by up to 25%. Manual cleaning is labor-intensive, dangerous, and inefficient for large-scale solar installations. The industry needed an autonomous solution that could navigate complex solar farm environments safely while performing precision cleaning operations.

Solution

Developed a fully autonomous robotic platform integrating cutting-edge computer vision, SLAM navigation, and mobile app control. The robot autonomously identifies solar panels, plans optimal cleaning paths, detects and avoids obstacles in real-time, and reports mission progress through a cloud-connected mobile application accessible from anywhere.

Technical Approach

The system architecture combines NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano for high-performance AI inference with OAK-D stereo depth cameras providing real-time 3D environmental understanding. Custom YOLOv8 models were trained on 5,000+ annotated images of solar panels in various conditions, achieving 95%+ detection accuracy across different lighting conditions, panel angles, and environmental scenarios.

SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) algorithms enable the robot to build real-time maps of solar farm layouts while tracking its precise position within the environment. The navigation stack implements dynamic path planning that adapts to moving obstacles, terrain changes, and mission requirements, ensuring safe and efficient operation in complex industrial settings.
