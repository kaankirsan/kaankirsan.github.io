Project Overview

Engineered precision mobile target system for defense industry warhead testing applications requiring automated movement, position tracking, and comprehensive safety protocols. Implemented SIL 2 certified safety systems ensuring operational safety in high-risk testing environments. Designed robust motion control platform capable of executing complex movement patterns with high precision and repeatability essential for accurate testing procedures.

Technical Approach

The mobile target system was architected around a safety-critical PLC platform meeting SIL 2 (Safety Integrity Level 2) requirements for defense industry applications. The control architecture incorporated redundant safety circuits, comprehensive fault detection, and fail-safe operational modes ensuring personnel protection and equipment integrity during all testing scenarios.

Motion control systems utilized high-precision servo drives and feedback systems providing accurate position control essential for repeatable testing procedures. Multi-axis coordination enabled complex target movement patterns simulating realistic operational scenarios. Position accuracy within millimeters was achieved through closed-loop control with real-time position feedback and advanced motion profiling.

The safety system design included multiple safety zones, emergency stop systems, and automated hazard detection ensuring safe operation in testing environments. Comprehensive interlocking prevented operation during unsafe conditions, with detailed diagnostic information aiding troubleshooting and system validation.

Automated tracking capabilities integrated position sensors, encoders, and timing systems providing precise control over target presentation during testing sequences. Programmable test scenarios allowed operators to configure complex movement patterns, speeds, and timing sequences meeting diverse testing requirements.

The HMI provided operators with comprehensive system monitoring, test sequence programming, safety status indication, and diagnostic information. Detailed logging capabilities recorded all test parameters, system status, and operational events supporting test documentation and regulatory compliance requirements.

Key Results & Impact

Achieving SIL 2 certification validated the system's safety-critical design rigor, meeting stringent safety requirements for defense industry testing applications. This certification provided confidence to operators and regulatory authorities that the system incorporated appropriate safety measures for high-risk testing environments.

Precision motion control capabilities enabled accurate and repeatable testing procedures essential for valid warhead testing results. Position accuracy, speed control, and timing precision met demanding defense industry requirements for test equipment performance and reliability.

The automated tracking and programmable test sequences improved testing efficiency by reducing manual setup time and ensuring consistent test execution. Complex movement patterns could be programmed, validated, and executed repeatedly with high precision supporting comprehensive testing programs.

Safety system implementation achieved zero safety incidents during extensive testing operations, demonstrating the effectiveness of the safety-critical design approach. Comprehensive interlocking, hazard detection, and fail-safe modes protected personnel and equipment throughout all operational phases.

The project demonstrated expertise in safety-critical systems engineering, precision motion control, and regulatory compliance essential for defense industry applications where reliability, safety, and performance are paramount requirements.
