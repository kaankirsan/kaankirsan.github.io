About Me

I'm Kaan Kırşan, a Robotics & Automation Engineer with a Bachelor's degree in Electrical-Electronics Engineering from Eskişehir Osmangazi University (2014-2019), specializing in Industrial Automation and Control Systems. With 5+ years of progressive experience at Doka Mühendislik in Eskişehir, Turkey, my career spans robotics, computer vision, motor control systems, PLC/HMI programming, IoT solutions, and energy storage systems.

My flagship achievement is developing an autonomous solar panel cleaning robot achieving 85% autonomy using NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano, computer vision (OpenCV, YOLOv5/v8), SLAM algorithms, and real-time obstacle detection with OAK-D depth cameras. I've also led the ABB welding cell motor integration delivering 40% production efficiency improvement, designed durability test automation platforms managing 50+ test scenarios with 35% time reduction, and implemented industrial communication infrastructure across 20+ machinery integrations achieving 99.5% system uptime.

I bring international experience through Erasmus programs in Latvia, Poland, and Lithuania, as well as participation in university UAV team projects. This global exposure has strengthened my ability to work in multicultural environments and manage international technical documentation. I'm passionate about solving complex engineering challenges through innovative robotics and automation, delivering quantifiable business value, and continuously expanding my expertise in autonomous systems and AI-driven solutions.
